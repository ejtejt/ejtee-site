About the Artist

Creative Designer & Lettering, stroke by stroke

Meet EJ, a Singapore-based designer who began his career after graduating in Graphic Design. Transitioning from product designer to bespoke type designer, he crafts unique pieces for corporate and private clients, working across mediums to elevate brands.

Embracing hand lettering as his passion, EJ finds joy in creating distinct artwork from scratch — including traditional sign painting. He shares his expertise through workshops, fostering a community of artists who share his enthusiasm for the craft.

EJ is dedicated to enriching the creative landscape, stroke by stroke.
